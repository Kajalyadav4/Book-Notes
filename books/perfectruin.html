Perfect Ruin - by Shanora Williams

ISBN: 9781432891435
Date read: 2024/02/18
How strongly I recommend it: 6/10

"Perfect Ruin" by Shanora Williams is a contemporary romance novel that follows the story of Payton and Dominic, two individuals with complicated pasts and intense chemistry.

Detailed notes

  • Setting: "Perfect Ruin" primarily unfolds in a contemporary urban setting.
  • Plot Overview:
    • Unexpected Encounter: Payton and Dominic meet under unexpected circumstances, immediately feeling a powerful connection despite their guarded natures.
    • Intense Chemistry: They're drawn to each other but struggle with their own inner demons and hesitations about relationships due to past experiences.
    • Emotional Turmoil: As they delve deeper into their budding relationship, both Payton and Dominic confront their past traumas and fears, affecting their ability to trust and love fully.
    • Navigating Challenges: Their journey together is fraught with challenges as they try to navigate their feelings, confront their insecurities, and build a future amidst their complex backgrounds.
  • Character Analysis:
    • Payton: A young woman with a tumultuous past, trying to rebuild her life and career in New York City.She evolves from a guarded individual with a troubled past to someone willing to take a chance on love and emotional vulnerability.
    • Dominic: A successful businessman with a reputation for being ruthless, harboring his own secrets and demons.He transforms from a closed-off businessman with a hardened exterior to someone capable of opening his heart and trusting again.
  • Themes Explored:
    • Emotional Healing: The novel delves into the healing power of love and trust, as both characters learn to open up and confront their emotional wounds.
    • Trust and Vulnerability: It explores the struggle of opening up to someone new after experiencing heartache and betrayal in the past.
    • Redemption and Second Chances: Both Payton and Dominic seek redemption and a chance at a better future, overcoming their past mistakes and pain.
  • Overall Impact:
    • Emotional Rollercoaster: "Perfect Ruin" is an emotionally charged love story, showcasing the struggles and triumphs of two individuals trying to find love and healing amidst their personal challenges.
    • Character-Driven Narrative: The story is driven by the personal growth and emotional journey of the characters as they navigate their complex pasts and the intricacies of their budding relationship.
"Perfect Ruin" captures the complexities of love, trust, and emotional healing, offering a passionate narrative that explores the vulnerabilities and strengths of its characters as they seek solace and happiness in each other.
